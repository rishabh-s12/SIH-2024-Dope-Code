Banned Substances in Olympic Sports

Doping substances, which are prohibited across all sports due to their performance-enhancing effects, vary based on the sport and the desired outcome (e.g., strength, endurance, recovery, or focus). Aquatic Sports

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Increase muscle mass and strength (e.g., Testosterone, Nandrolone).
  • Erythropoietin (EPO): Enhances endurance by increasing red blood cell production.
  • Stimulants: Improve focus and reduce fatigue (e.g., Amphetamines, Ephedrine).
  • Beta-2 Agonists: Improve breathing (e.g., Clenbuterol).
  • Diuretics and Masking Agents: Used to quickly lose weight or mask the presence of other drugs.

Athletics (Track and Field)

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Enhance speed and power in sprints and strength in throws.
  • EPO: Boosts endurance for long-distance events.
  • Human Growth Hormone (HGH): Promotes muscle growth and recovery.
  • Stimulants: Improve alertness and reduce fatigue, useful in high-intensity events.
  • Beta Blockers: Used to reduce tremors and heart rate, beneficial for precision sports like shooting.

Ball Sports

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Increase strength and power, especially useful in contact sports (e.g., Rugby, Basketball).
  • Stimulants: Enhance focus and aggression, useful across team sports.
  • Diuretics: Used to meet weight categories or mask other substances

Combat Sports

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Improve strength and recovery, crucial in combat situations.
  • Diuretics and Masking Agents: Used to make weight categories or hide other drug use.
  • Stimulants: Increase aggression and alertness.

Cycling

Common Doping Substances:

  • EPO: Widely used to enhance endurance by increasing red blood cell count.
  • Anabolic Steroids: Used for strength in sprints and recovery.
  • HGH: Helps in muscle recovery and repair.
  • Blood Doping: Increases oxygen delivery to muscles.
  • Stimulants: Boost energy levels during long races.

Gymnastics

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Enhance muscle strength for explosive movements.
  • Beta Blockers: Reduce anxiety and steady hands for routines.
  • Stimulants: Increase focus and energy.

Weightlifting

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Vital for increasing muscle mass, strength, and power.
  • HGH: Promotes muscle growth and speeds up recovery.
  • Stimulants: Enhance strength output during lifts.
  • Diuretics: Used to make weight classes and mask other substances.

Modern Penthalon

Common Doping Substances:

  • Anabolic Steroids: Improve strength and speed across various events.
  • EPO: Enhances endurance for the running and swimming components.
  • Beta Blockers: Useful for the shooting component to reduce heart rate and anxiety.
  • Stimulants: Increase overall performance and focus.

Winter Sports

Common Doping Substances:

  • EPO: Important for endurance sports like cross-country skiing.
  • Anabolic Steroids: Useful in strength-focused sports like bobsleigh and alpine skiing.
  • HGH: Enhances muscle recovery and growth.
  • Stimulants: Improve focus and reaction times across many sports.
  • Beta Blockers: Used in sports requiring precision like biathlon.

Disclaimer: The use of any of these substances without a Therapeutic Use Exemption (TUE) is considered illegal and may lead to sanctions, including disqualification and bans from competition.
